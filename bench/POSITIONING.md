# Lab scope

This lab exists for DocPull engineering decisions. It is not an independent
public leaderboard.

The relevant product boundary is reproducible context dependency management:
known-source extraction, bounded crawling, document parsing, artifact packing,
change detection, local retrieval, lifecycle controls, and policy behavior.
Open-web search and fixed-corpus research are separate lanes with separate
eligible systems. Unsupported lanes do not authorize new DocPull product APIs
or restoration of removed search/research surfaces.
