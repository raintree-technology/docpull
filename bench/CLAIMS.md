# Claim policy

Generated benchmark output is data and methodology, not marketing copy.

- Never claim a global winner or aggregate different lanes.
- Never use provisional historical bundles as current evidence.
- Never convert `unsupported` into a loss.
- Qualify live results with suite/protocol hashes, check dates, environment,
  cache class, costs, and limitations.
- Keep narrative findings separate and require human review.
- Require independent reproduction or separately held evidence before making a
  competitive claim.
